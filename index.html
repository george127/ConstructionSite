    who we are

    Welcome to Ted-Nomex Construction Company! With over 12 years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as a leading construction firm dedicated to delivering exceptional building solutions. Our journey began with a vision to transform the construction landscape through innovative practices, superior craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to quality.


    Our Mission

    At Ted-Nomex, our mission is to build lasting relationships with our clients by consistently exceeding their expectations and delivering projects that stand the test of time.
    We strive to create sustainable, functional, and aesthetically pleasing spaces that enhance the lives of the communities we serve.


    What We Do
    Ted-Nomex offers a comprehensive range of construction services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Whether it's a custom home, a commercial complex, or an industrial facility, we bring the same level of dedication and attention to detail to every project.

    Residential Construction: Custom homes, renovations, and multi-family housing.

    Commercial Construction: Office buildings, retail spaces, and mixed-use developments.

    Industrial Construction: Warehouses, manufacturing plants, and distribution centers.

    Design and Build: Integrated services from concept to completion, ensuring seamless project execution.


    Our Values
    Quality: We are committed to delivering the highest quality in every project we undertake. Our meticulous attention to detail and use of premium materials ensure superior results.

    Integrity: Honesty and transparency are the cornerstones of our business. We believe in building trust through open communication and ethical practices.

    Innovation: We embrace modern construction techniques and technologies to enhance efficiency, safety, and sustainability.

    Safety: The well-being of our team and clients is our top priority. We adhere to stringent safety protocols and provide ongoing training to our staff.

    Sustainability: We are dedicated to sustainable building practices that minimize environmental impact and promote energy efficiency.


    Our Team
    Our success is driven by a talented team of professionals who bring expertise, passion, and creativity to every project. From architects and engineers to project managers and skilled laborers, our team collaborates seamlessly to ensure the successful delivery of each project. We invest in continuous training and development to keep our team at the forefront of industry advancements.

    Why Choose Us

    Experience: Decades of experience in the construction industry.

    Reputation: A proven track record of successful projects and satisfied clients.

    Customization: Tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each client.

    Customer Service: Exceptional support and communication throughout the project lifecycle.

    Sustainability: Commitment to eco-friendly practices and energy-efficient designs.


    Community Involvement

    At Ted-Nomex, we believe in giving back to the community. We actively participate in local projects and support initiatives that contribute to the development and well-being of the communities we serve.


    Contact Us

    Ready to bring your vision to life? Contact us today to discuss your project and discover how Ted-Nomex can turn your dreams into reality.
